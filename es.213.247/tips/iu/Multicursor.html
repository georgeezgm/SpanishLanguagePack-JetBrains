In IntelliJ IDEA Community, you can use multiple carets. Press and hold Shift+Alt (on Windows and Linux) / ⇧⌥ (on macOS) and then click at different positions to set additional carets in the editor.

You can then press Shift with the left or right arrow keys to select multiple text fragments.

Press &shortcut:EditorEscape; to remove all the carets except for the recently added one.
