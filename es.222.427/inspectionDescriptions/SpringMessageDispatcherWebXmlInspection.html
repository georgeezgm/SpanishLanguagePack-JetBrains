web.xml에 등록되었으나 웹 루트에 필요한 XML 애플리케이션 컨텍스트가 없는 MessageDispatcherServlet 서블릿을 보고합니다.

web.xml 예시:


<web-app>
  <servlet>
    <servlet-name>spring-ws</servlet-name>  <!-- Cannot find application context WEB-INF/spring-ws-servlet.xml -->
    <servlet-class>org.springframework.ws.transport.http.MessageDispatcherServlet</servlet-class>
    <load-on-startup>1</load-on-startup>
  </servlet>
</web-app>
